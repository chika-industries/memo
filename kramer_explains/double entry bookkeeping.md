### Scene 1: Double-Entry Bookkeeping

**Setting:** Jerry's apartment. Jerry is reading a book. Kramer bursts in, holding a cheap ledger book.

**Kramer:** Jerry! I figured it out! The secret to wealth! It's all here! *(He slams the ledger on the table)*

**Jerry:** Is that a coloring book for people who really love spreadsheets?

**Kramer:** No, no, no. This is my new accounting system. Double-entry bookkeeping! My dad told me about it. It's what all the rich guys use.

**Jerry:** Your dad also told you that eating a whole stick of butter was a "Polish energy bar." I'm skeptical.

**Kramer:** Listen! Double-entry! You keep two books, see? *(He opens the ledger, revealing two columns)*

**Jerry:** Two columns. That's... that's usually how a ledger works.

**Kramer:** No, not two columns! Two *books*! Two separate, distinct ledgers. Book Number One... *(He holds up the ledger)* ...is for me. It's the truth. It says, "Kramer, April 15th, found $20 on the street. Spent $18 on a really nice Hawaiian shirt. Profit: $2 and a great look."

**Jerry:** Okay... so what's in Book Number Two?

**Kramer:** *(Pulls a second, identical ledger from his jacket)* Book Number Two! For the tax people. It says, "Kramer Enterprises, April 15th, consulting fees: $20. Operating expenses: shirt rental for client meeting, $18. Net income: $2. Please audit this, it's very boring."

**Jerry:** So double-entry bookkeeping, to you, means one book for reality and one book for the government.

**Kramer:** That's right! One for me, one for them. Double-entry! You get it! See, the rich guys, they don't get rich by telling everyone everything. It's a system, Jerry!

**Jerry:** It's a system, all right. It's called fraud. It's a system that ends with you sharing a cell with a guy named "The Refrigerator."

**Kramer:** *(Waving him off)* Nah, they'll never find Book One. I'm gonna keep it in a safe place.

**Jerry:** Where? The garbage chute?

**Kramer:** *(Gasps)* How'd you know? I gotta move it!

*(Kramer runs out, leaving both ledgers on Jerry's table. Jerry stares at them.)*

**Jerry:** And... they're both still here. I think he's really mastered the concept.
