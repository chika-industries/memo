# 🏭 chika-industries / memo

> A personal “virtual company” where ideas are the product, curiosity is the strategy, and AI is the intern that never sleeps.

---

## What is this?

This repository is a growing collection of thoughts, notes, and experiments around:

* 💰 Personal finance
* 📈 Investing & portfolio strategy
* 🧮 Accounting concepts (RoE, DuPont, etc.)
* 🧠 Mental models for growth and decision-making

Originally, this started as a **serious, structured knowledge base** — clean explanations, AI-assisted summaries, textbook-style clarity.

But somewhere along the way, that got... boring.

---

## The Shift

Recently, the project has taken a turn:

> What if complex financial concepts were explained like a chaotic sitcom character just burst into your apartment and started ranting?

So now:

* 📊 **RoE gets explained with dramatic flair**
* 🧾 **Accounting becomes a performance**
* 🤯 **Finance concepts become stories, not lectures**

Yes — this is slowly becoming:

> **“Kramer explains finance”**

And honestly, it's just better.
